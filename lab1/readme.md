## Sorting water pumps 
**Attributes of class**
* _power in watt_
* _brand_
* _liter per hour_

**Sort methods:**
* Insertion sort 
    * `Descending`
    * `Sorting pumps by power in watts`
    * `Complexity: O(n^2)`
* Heap sort
    * `Ascending`
    * `Sorting pumps by volume of water`
    * `Complexity: O(n*log(n))`
